Overview

Tired of spending tedious hours chopping down every single log block? The Tree Chopper Mod for Minecraft Bedrock Edition revolutionizes wood gathering, letting you fell entire trees with a single swing of your axe! This quality-of-life mod is an essential addition for survival worlds, turning the labor of deforestation into a swift and satisfying experience.

Simply break the bottom log of any naturally spawned tree with an axe, and the rest of the connected log blocks—and optionally the leaves—will instantly be cleared for you. The mod is fully customizable via an in-game settings UI, giving you total control over speed, durability, and more.

Key Features

  • One-Click Tree Felling (Destroy all logs and leaves with one break)
  • In-Game Settings UI via Guide Book for full control
  • Auto-Replant toggle for sustainable forestry
  • Leaf Destruction toggle (Clear floating leaves instantly)
  • Durability Control (Respects axe durability, adjustable)
  • Custom particle effects and soAnti-Grief Logic (Skips player-placed blocks)unds
  • No Need of Any *Experimental* Features

Installation Instructions

  1. Make sure you have Minecraft Forge 1.20.1 installed
  2. Download the Epic Tools mod file (.jar)
  3. Navigate to your Minecraft mods folder
  4. Place the downloaded .jar file in the mods folder
  5. Launch Minecraft with the Forge profile
  6. Enjoy crafting epic tools!

System Requirements

  • Minecraft Version: 1.20.1
  • Mod Loader: Forge 47.0.0 or higher
  • RAM: At least 4GB allocated to Minecraft
  • Compatible with: OptiFine, JEI, and most major mods
